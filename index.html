About the Imprint

Holdfast Press publishes literary fantasy for readers of Stephen R. Lawhead, J.R.R. Tolkien, and C.S. Lewis — stories in which the weight of the past is real, the friendships are the soul of the book, and the world is stranger and older than it appears.

The Keepers is the first series. Future series follow. The imprint is for the literary-fantasy bookshelf, and for readers who want to carry something home.
