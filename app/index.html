A dedicated reading and listening app for The Keepers and every series that follows — built by Holdfast Press for readers who want the book with them everywhere.

What it does

Read

The full text of every published book — clean, distraction-free, formatted for long reading sessions. Chapter navigation, adjustable type, bookmarks.

Listen

High-quality narration with synchronized text highlighting — follow along as you listen or close your eyes and let the book run. Adjustable speed. Chapter and paragraph navigation.

Sync

Your position, bookmarks, and reading progress follow you across every device — phone, tablet, browser. Pick up where you left off, always.

The full catalog

Every Holdfast Press title in one place. As new books in The Keepers series publish, they appear in your library automatically — no manual downloads, no hunting for files.

Add StoryReader to your device

StoryReader installs straight from your browser — no app store, no download. Open app.holdfastpress.com on the device you want it on, then follow the steps for your platform.

Open the app

iPhone & iPad

Safari

  1. Open app.holdfastpress.com in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button — the square with the up arrow.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.

On iPhone and iPad it must be Safari — Chrome can’t install web apps.

Android

Chrome

  1. Open app.holdfastpress.com in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  3. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app.
  4. Confirm.

Windows

Chrome or Edge

  1. Open app.holdfastpress.com.
  2. Click the install icon at the right end of the address bar.
  3. Or use ⋮ → Install this site as an app (Edge: ⋯ → Apps).
  4. Click Install.

Mac

Chrome or Safari

  1. Open app.holdfastpress.com.
  2. Chrome: click the install icon in the address bar, or ⋮ → Install…
  3. Safari (macOS Sonoma and later): File → Add to Dock.

Prefer not to install? StoryReader runs just as well right in your browser.
